Professional feedback

The donation process from your side is quite simple: you place your donation and that’s it!

From our side we will convert your kindly donated dollars into new literature books using the 1BOOK4LIFE platform. In a few days you will receive one email from us detailing how many books were bought (with each title and price payed for), where they will be delivered (the school name and address in Brazil), and in which class and grade the benefited students attend to.

After that, 1BOOK4LIFE will also send you emails containing important information about your donation, how to share it with friends, your account credentials – so you can follow everything that happens -, and much more.

No student is left behind

All the students attending to the school get books! Every one of them! 100%!

Just imagine: one day the students have no literature books. Most of them won’t have access to a book like this their whole lives. However, thanks to you and the other donors, everything changes. All the students, at the same day, get brand new literature books! The books belong to the student, not to the school, to take them home, share among family, friends and classmates. They way they want.

Different books. The same joy!

Every student gets a set of two brand new literature books, specially chosen to enjoyed. The idea is to give books that the students will love to read.

Picture this if you were one of those kids: all your classmates have beautiful, brand new books, different from yours. If you enjoy reading your two books, why not exchange them with a friend of your school?

Different books that can be shared. Different stories, emotions and one thing in common: they are fun!
